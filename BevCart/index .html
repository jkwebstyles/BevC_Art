Hi Welcome to BevC's-Art,

Hi! I'm Bev, a South Somerset based, coloured pencil artist, specialising in creating realistic pet portraits that you can treasure for a lifetime.

As a proud mum to three wonderful children, who have grown up and flown the nest. I am also lucky to have three, gorgeous dogs, who are all full of character, along with a super big horse, who in turn, keep my husband and I, entertained. With these precious animals, I know how important our family and pets are to us;

I want to help other pet lovers, where ever they may be, remember their much-loved animals forever, with a lifelike portrait that can be kept and cherished for years to come.
