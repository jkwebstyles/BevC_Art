BevC's-Art Price List

Book Your Slot I ask for a 25% booking fee to secure your portrait space, with the full balance due on completion.

Send Me Your Photos Where possible, I work from good quality, digital photos, and I'm happy to receive as many as you'd like to send. We then work together to choose the best one for the portrait.

It's Drawing Time! My portraits are created using artist quality lightfast Coloured Pencils. I use archival acid free paper, and each portrait is professionally mounted ready for you to frame.

Mounting & Shipping My framers, Westgate Fine Art, look after all my work. Your portrait will be professionally mounted by them before shipping, using acid free thick mount board, protected with acid free Acetate ready for you to have framed when you receive it. Global shipping is included in the prices shown below.
